## Part A — Rules you must know

### 1. BODMAS order
**B**rackets → **O**f (powers/roots) → **D**ivision & **M**ultiplication (left to right) → **A**ddition & **S**ubtraction (left to right)

### 4. Percentage ↔ fraction (speed)

| % | Fraction | % | Fraction |
|---|----------|---|----------|
| 50% | 1/2 | 20% | 1/5 |
| 25% | 1/4 | 10% | 1/10 |
| 12.5% | 1/8 | 5% | 1/20 |

**Trick:** 12.5% of 800 = 800 ÷ 8 = 100

### 5. Quick multiplication
- ×5 → ×10 then ÷2  
- ×25 → ×100 then ÷4  
- ×125 → ×1000 then ÷8  

### 6. Approximation (exam style)
When options are far apart, round:
- 39 × 21 ≈ 40 × 20 = 800  
- 198 ÷ 19.6 ≈ 200 ÷ 20 = 10  

### 7. Common mistakes (avoid)
- Squaring **sum**: (a+b)² ≠ a² + b²  
- Negative × negative = **positive**  
- ÷ by fraction → **multiply by reciprocal**: 12 ÷ ¾ = 12 × 4/3 = 16  

### 8. Worked examples

**Ex 1:** 15% of 240 + 25% of 160 = 36 + 40 = **76**

**Ex 2:** 864 ÷ 12 × 3 = 72 × 3 = **216**

**Ex 3:** 45² − 44² = (45−44)(45+44) = **89**

**Ex 4:** ∛2197 + √289 = 13 + 17 = **30**
