# Reasoning — Puzzle Set (Floor + Box) — दिन 9

> **સૂચના (Gujarati):** કાગળ પર ટેબલ બનાવો. Set 1 ≈ 25 મિન, Set 2 ≈ 20 મિન.

**भाषा (सामग्री):** हिंदी · **समय:** 60 मिन

---

## भाग A — पद्धति

1. Floor: ground = floor 1, top = floor 8  
2. **Immediately above** = +1 floor  
3. Box: position **1 = top**, **5 = bottom**  
4. हर clue solve करते ही table update करें

---

## Set 1 — Floor puzzle

**8 floors (1–8), 8 persons: A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H**

1. A is on floor **5**  
2. B is **immediately above** A  
3. D is on floor **3**  
4. F is **immediately above** D  
5. C is on floor **7**  
6. H is on floor **1**  
7. E is on floor **8**  
8. G is on floor **2**

**प्रश्न:** 1) B की floor? 2) F की floor? 3) G की floor? 4) C और A के बीच floors? 5) Top पर कौन? 6) Floor 4 पर कौन? 7) D के ऊपर कौन? 8) H के ऊपर कौन?

---

## Set 2 — Box puzzle

**5 boxes: P, Q, R, S, T** (top to bottom = position 1 to 5)  
**Colours:** Red, Blue, Green, White, Yellow

1. **Q** is at the **top** and is **Yellow**  
2. **T** is at the **bottom** and is **Blue**  
3. **P** is **Red** and at **position 2**  
4. **R** is **immediately below** P and is **Green**  
5. **S** is **White** and at **position 4**

**प्रश्न:** 9) Top box? 10) P की colour? 11) S की colour? 12) Position 2? 13) Bottom box? 14) R की colour? 15) Position 4?
