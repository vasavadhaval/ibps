Customers prefer digital channels, **(4)______** many still visit branches for complex services. The management believes that strong cybersecurity is **(5)______** optional. In interviews, candidates are often asked about core banking systems and basic networking concepts. A good IT officer should know how to troubleshoot hardware, software, and connectivity issues **(6)______**. Banks also test logical ability and English, **(7)______** communication is important in every department. Aspirants should practice mocks regularly **(8)______** they can manage time in the actual exam. **(9)______** the syllabus is large, daily study is more effective than last-minute cramming. With consistent effort, even candidates from non-English backgrounds can improve **(10)______**.
