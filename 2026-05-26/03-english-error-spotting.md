# English — Error Spotting (Mixed) — Day 6

> **સૂચના (Gujarati):** પરીક્ષામાં **એક ભાગ** (A/B/C/D) માં ભૂલ — અથવા underline ભાગ બદલો.

**Content:** English · **Time:** 45 min

---

## Part A — Quick rules (10 min)

| Error type | Wrong | Right |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Subject-verb | He go | He goes |
| Tense | since 2020 + past | since + present perfect |
| Preposition | discuss about | discuss |
| Article | a honest | an honest |
| Plural | informations | information |
| Pronoun | Each of students have | has |
| Parallel | reading, to write | reading, writing |
| Double negative | don't know nothing | don't know anything |

**Method:** Read (A)(B)(C)(D) — one part wrong OR "No error"

---
