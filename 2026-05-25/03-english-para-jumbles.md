**Content:** English · **Time:** 45 min

---

## Part A — Method (10 min)

### Step 1 — Find anchors
- **Opening:** Definition, general statement, no "he/she/this/therefore" at start often  
- **Closing:** Conclusion, "thus", "hence", "finally", result  
- **Pairs:** pronoun **this/they** needs noun before · **Therefore** follows reason

### Step 2 — Link words
| First | Then |
|-------|------|
| However, But | contrast sentence before |
| Therefore, Thus, Hence | reason before |
| Also, Moreover | similar topic before |
| For example | general statement before |

### Step 3 — Test order
Read A-B-C-D aloud — smooth flow = correct

### Banking topics
Economy, digital banking, RBI, exam strategy — same logic
