# English — Phrase Replacement / Word Swap (Day 7)

> **સૂચના (Gujarati):** Underline ભાગને સારો વિકલ્પથી બદલો — grammar + meaning.

**Content:** English · **Time:** 45 min

---

## Part A — Method

1. Read full sentence — meaning clear?  
2. Check **grammar** (tense, preposition, article)  
3. Check **collocation** (*depend on*, *in spite of*)  
4. Two blanks sometimes — **both** must fit

**Common swaps:** affect/effect · lend/borrow · rise/raise · among/between

---

## Part B — 20 questions

*Choose the best replacement for the **bold** part OR the correct pair.*

1. The team **has been worked** on the project since Monday.  
   - (a) has worked  (b) have worked  (c) was working  (d) had worked

2. He **insisted for** paying the full amount.  
   - (a) insisted on  (b) insisted to  (c) insisted at  (d) insisted with

3. The officer **explained about** the new policy to customers.  
   - (a) explained  (b) explained on  (c) explained for  (d) explained into

4. She is **senior than** him in rank.  
   - (a) senior to  (b) more senior  (c) senior from  (d) No change

5. The bank **laid** strict rules for loan approval.  
   - (a) laid down  (b) lied  (c) laid up  (d) lain

6. **Despite of** rain, the branch remained open.  
   - (a) Despite  (b) In spite  (c) Although of  (d) Despite of (no change)

7. He **knows to solve** complex network issues.  
   - (a) knows how to solve  (b) know solving  (c) knows solve  (d) knowing solve

8. The data was **comprised of** ten fields.  
   - (a) comprised  (b) composed of  (c) comprised with  (d) both b or comprised (exam: **composed of** / **comprised** ten fields)

9. Please ** revert back** with your documents.  
   - (a) revert  (b) reply back  (c) return back  (d) No change

10. The manager **prevented him to enter** the server room.  
    - (a) prevented him from entering  (b) prevented him for enter  (c) prevented him enter  (d) prevented him to entering

11. **Each of the candidates** have submitted forms.  
    - (a) has submitted  (b) have submit  (c) are submitted  (d) No change

12. The RBI **has raised** the repo rate **last week**.  
    - (a) raised  (b) has been raising  (c) raises  (d) had raised

13. He **suggested me to apply** for the post.  
    - (a) suggested that I apply  (b) suggested me applying  (c) suggested to me apply  (d) suggested me apply

14. The number of fraud cases **have risen**.  
    - (a) has risen  (b) have rose  (c) has rose  (d) No change

15. She **told that** she will come tomorrow.  
    - (a) said that  (b) told  (c) said  (d) told to

16. **Between** the five banks, SBI is largest.  
    - (a) Among  (b) Between each  (c) Amid  (d) No change

17. He is **eligible for apply** the exam.  
    - (a) eligible to apply for  (b) eligible for applying  (c) eligible apply  (d) eligible to applying

18. The clerk **accommodate** the customer politely.  
    - (a) accommodated  (b) accommodating  (c) accommodates  (d) accommodation

19. **Not only** he cleared Prelims **but also** Mains.  
    - (a) Not only did he clear Prelims but also Mains  (b) Not only he cleared Prelims but he also Mains  (c) Not only he clear  (d) No change

20. The report is **more better** than last year.  
    - (a) better  (b) much better  (c) more good  (d) best
