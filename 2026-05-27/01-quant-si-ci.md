## भाग A — नियम

### Simple Interest (SI)
**SI = (P × R × T) / 100**  
P = मूलधन, R = दर % प्रति वर्ष, T = समय (वर्ष)

**Amount = P + SI**

**उदा:** P=5000, R=10%, T=2 → SI = 5000×10×2/100 = **1000** · Amount = **6000**

### Compound Interest (CI)
**A = P (1 + R/100)ⁿ**  
CI = A − P · **n** = समय (वर्ष, compounding period)

| Compounding | सूत्र |
|-------------|--------|
| Annually (वार्षिक) | n = years |
| Half-yearly | R/2, n = 2×years |
| Quarterly | R/4, n = 4×years |

**उदा:** P=10000, R=20%, 2 years CI  
A = 10000×(1.2)² = 10000×1.44 = **14400** · CI = **4400**

### SI vs CI (2 years same R)
**CI − SI (2 yr) = P × (R/100)²**  
**उदा:** P=1000, R=10% → diff = 1000×0.01 = **10**

### तेज़ ट्रिक
- **Double in SI:** T = 100/R years (R% पर)  
- **Double in CI (annual):** ~72/R years (rule of 72 — approximate)  
- **Same P,R,T:** CI > SI (T>1 year)

### Installment / exam mix
कभी **effective rate** या **difference** पूछते हैं — पहले SI निकालो, फिर CI
