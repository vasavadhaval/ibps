# English — Cloze Test (Day 4)

> **સૂચના (Gujarati):** Cloze = પેસેજમાં ખાલી જગ્યા ભરો. **English** only — પરીક્ષામાં વારંવાર આવે.

**Content:** English · **Time:** 45 min

---

## Part A — How to solve cloze (5 min)

1. **Read full passage once** — topic samajho (banking/economy common).  
2. **Grammar first:** article (a/an/the), preposition, tense.  
3. **Meaning second:** which word fits context.  
4. **Link words:** *however, therefore, although* — sentence connection.  
5. **Eliminate** 2 options fast in MCQ cloze.
