# Reasoning — Linear Seating — दिन 10

> **સૂચના (Gujarati):** એક row ડાયગ્રામ — left/right અને facing (north/south) લખો.

**भाषा (सामग्री):** हिंदी · **समय:** 60 मिन

---

## भाग A — नियम

### Single row — all face same direction
- **Left/right** = जैसे वे बैठे (आमतौर पर same as reader)  
- **A, B के बीच 2 लोग** = positions differ by 3  
- **Immediate left of X** = X की position − 1 (left end = 1)

### Two rows — opposite facing
- Row 1 faces **North**, Row 2 faces **South**  
- **A sits opposite B** = same position number, different row  
- **Left of A (row 1)** vs **right of B (row 2)** — direction swap

### Steps
1. Fixed positions (ends, middle, “third from left”)  
2. “Between A and B” / “not at ends”  
3. Cross-check all clues

---

## Set 1 — Single row (7 persons, face North)

**Positions 1–7 left to right:** P, Q, R, S, T, U, V (names once each)

1. S is at the **centre** (position 4).  
2. P is at the **left end**.  
3. Q is **immediately right of** P.  
4. T is **immediately left of** S.  
5. U is **not** at any end.  
6. R is **immediately right of** S.  
7. V is at the **right end**.

**प्रश्न 1–8:**

1. Position 3 पर कौन?  
2. T की position?  
3. R और U में कौन right side?  
4. P और V के बीच कितने लोग?  
5. S के immediate right?  
6. Left end पर?  
7. U की position?  
8. Q और T के बीच कौन?

## Set 2 — Two rows (4 + 4, opposite)

**Row 1 (North):** A, B, C, D — positions 1–4 left to right  
**Row 2 (South):** W, X, Y, Z — positions 1–4 left to right  
**Opposite:** same position number

1. A is at position 1 (Row 1).  
2. **Z sits opposite A.**  
3. B is **immediately right of** A.  
4. **Y sits opposite B.**  
5. C is at position 3 (Row 1).  
6. **X sits opposite C.**  
7. D is **immediately right of** C.  
8. **W sits opposite D.**

**प्रश्न 9–15:**

9. Row 2 position 1 पर?  
10. Y की Row 2 position?  
11. C का opposite?  
12. B का opposite?  
13. Row 1 position 4?  
14. Z और B same position number?  
15. A और X opposite? (True/False)
