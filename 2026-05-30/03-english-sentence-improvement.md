# English — Sentence Improvement — Day 10

> **સૂચના (Gujarati):** Underline ભાગ સુધારો — grammar + meaning બંને.

**Content:** English · **Time:** 45 min

---

## Part A — Method

1. Read full sentence  
2. Check **tense, subject-verb, preposition, article**  
3. Pick option that is **grammatically correct** and **natural**  
4. If all wrong, choose **No improvement**

---

## Part B — 20 questions

*Choose the best replacement for the bold part.*

1. She **has went** to the branch yesterday.  
   - (a) went  (b) has gone  (c) had went  (d) No improvement

2. The officer **insisted to** meet the manager.  
   - (a) insisted on  (b) insisted for  (c) insisted at  (d) No improvement

3. He is **more smarter** than his colleague.  
   - (a) smarter  (b) most smart  (c) more smart  (d) No improvement

4. **Each of the students** have submitted the form.  
   - (a) has submitted  (b) are submitted  (c) have submit  (d) No improvement

5. The data **were** corrupted last night.  
   - (a) was  (b) are  (c) have been  (d) No improvement

6. I **have seen** him two days ago.  
   - (a) saw  (b) see  (c) had seen  (d) No improvement

7. He **discussed about** the loan with the customer.  
   - (a) discussed  (b) discussed on  (c) discusses about  (d) No improvement

8. The team ** comprises of** five members.  
   - (a) comprises  (b) comprise of  (c) is comprising  (d) No improvement

9. She **knows to solve** network issues quickly.  
   - (a) knows how to solve  (b) know solving  (c) knows solve  (d) No improvement

10. **Between** the three options, this is best.  
    - (a) Among  (b) Between each  (c) Amidst  (d) No improvement

11. He **prevented me to enter** the server room.  
    - (a) prevented me from entering  (b) prevented me for enter  (c) prevented me enter  (d) No improvement

12. The RBI **has raised** rates **last month**.  
    - (a) raised  (b) raises  (c) had raised  (d) No improvement

13. She **told that** she will come tomorrow.  
    - (a) said that  (b) told  (c) says that  (d) No improvement

14. He is **senior than** me in rank.  
    - (a) senior to  (b) more senior  (c) senior from  (d) No improvement

15. **Despite of** rain, the exam was held.  
    - (a) Despite  (b) In spite  (c) Although of  (d) No improvement

16. The number of applications **have increased**.  
    - (a) has increased  (b) have rose  (c) are increasing  (d) No improvement

17. He **suggested me to apply** for IT Officer.  
    - (a) suggested that I apply  (b) suggested me applying  (c) suggested to me apply  (d) No improvement

18. This is **more better** than the previous version.  
    - (a) better  (b) much better  (c) more good  (d) No improvement

19. **Not only** he cleared Prelims **but also** Mains.  
    - (a) Not only did he clear Prelims but also Mains  (b) Not only he cleared Prelims but he also cleared Mains  (c) Not only he clear  (d) No improvement

20. Please **revert back** with your documents.  
    - (a) revert  (b) reply back  (c) return back  (d) No improvement
